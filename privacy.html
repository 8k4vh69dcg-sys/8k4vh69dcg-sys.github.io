Pixelle Workflow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-23

This Privacy Policy explains how Pixelle Workflow handles data when used to generate short-form videos and connect to third-party publishing platforms such as TikTok.

1. Data Collected

The service may process account authorization data, captions, prompts, generated media, publishing metadata, and post status information needed to complete creator workflows.

2. TikTok Authorization Data

When you connect a TikTok account, the service may receive and use authorization credentials such as access tokens, refresh tokens, scope information, and account identifiers provided through TikTok’s OAuth flow.

3. How Data Is Used

Data is used only to support requested creator actions, including generating videos, preparing captions, uploading content, checking publishing status, and retrieving post metrics where authorized.

4. Data Sharing

The service shares data only with the infrastructure and platform endpoints required to perform requested actions, such as AI model providers, media generation providers, and connected social platforms. The service does not sell personal data.

5. Data Storage

Workflow files, generated videos, run manifests, and publishing state may be stored locally or in the deployment environment used by the operator. Tokens and credentials should be stored securely and handled on the server side whenever possible.

6. User Control

You may stop using the service at any time and revoke connected platform access through the destination platform’s app authorization settings when supported.

7. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored tokens, workflow metadata, and generated content. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

The service is not intended for use by children and should not be used to knowingly collect personal information from minors in violation of applicable law.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the service after an update means the revised policy will apply.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the operator through the project or deployment channel that provided access to Pixelle Workflow.
